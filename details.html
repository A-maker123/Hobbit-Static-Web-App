Bilbo Baggins

Burglar, storyteller, and the most unlikely hero in Middle earth.

Universe: The Hobbit

Species: Hobbit

Role: Burglar turned hero

Power level: Medium

Backstory

Bilbo Baggins lives a quiet and predictable life in the Shire until Gandalf and a company of dwarves show up at his door. He is recruited as the group’s “burglar” on a quest to reclaim the dwarven homeland of Erebor from the dragon Smaug. The journey forces Bilbo to adapt, take risks, and discover bravery he did not know he had.

Abilities and skills

  • Stealth: surprisingly good at sneaking when it counts
  • Quick thinking: talks his way out of trouble and makes clever bargains
  • Courage under pressure: grows into a leader in critical moments
  • Ring bearer: gains invisibility through the One Ring, with consequences

First appearance

The Hobbit by J. R. R. Tolkien, first published in 1937.

Personality traits

Clever Loyal Anxious but brave Curious Comfort loving
